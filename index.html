FAQ

Everything you need to know.

How far in advance should I book?

At least 2–3 weeks in advance is recommended, especially during holidays and peak travel seasons.

Do you travel outside Orlando?

Yes. We service Central Florida and select destinations including Tampa, Cocoa Beach, Melbourne, and Miami. Travel fees may apply.

Is the hotel included?

No. Clients book and pay for their hotel, resort, Airbnb, or accommodation separately.

Is the deposit refundable?

Deposits are non-refundable but may be transferred to a new approved date if rescheduling is needed.
